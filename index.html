About Me

What I Do

Hi! I'm Erik and I'm a Milwaukee-based software developer. I design, develop, deliver, and enhance software and online products across multiple platforms. I enjoy working across the full stack. With an obsessive attention to detail and strong ability to think through problems, I use my knowledge and skills to add value to any team or project I am with. I also drink coffee like it's my job.

What I Love

Leonardo DaVinci said, "Simplicity is the ultimate form of sophistication." Inspired by this, I think there is a beauty in simplicity, in clarity, in efficiency. But I feel true simplicity is derived from so much more than just the absence of clutter. It's about bringing order to complexity. Good code is simple. It is efficient. It is beautiful. I strive to create that.

Where I'm From

I live in Wisconsin, just outside of Milwaukee. This is home to me. But despite my midwest roots, I have traveled the world and experienced many amazing places and cultures. I think seeing the world from a foreign place breaks me from the constraints of familiarity, allowing me to see things I normally would not have. I like to take a similar approach when faced with any problem-solving situation.

What I Know

As a developer, I know a number of programming languages allowing me to produce both on the front and back-end. I know how to work through all stages of a development project. I know how to think logically, but also, creatively. I know how to collaborate with others and communicate to effectively express my ideas. I also have experience managing several staff members and projects across multiple platforms.

I don't, however, know anything about Pilosebaceous structures, Extensor distribution, or any other area of scientific study that have more than five syllables. That, I'm afraid, would be my sister.
